## The Practice

Deposplit's implementation of Shamir's Secret Sharing is based on [Privy's TypeScript implementation](https://github.com/privy-io/shamir-secret-sharing) and was ported to [Kotlin](https://github.com/Deposplit/Android/tree/main/hexagon/src/main/kotlin/com/deposplit/shamir) and [Swift](https://github.com/Deposplit/iOS/tree/main/hexagon/Sources/shamir) as well as [Scala](https://github.com/Deposplit/deposplit.com/tree/main/hexagons/phon/src/main/scala/shamir) by [Claude Code](https://www.anthropic.com/product/claude-code).
