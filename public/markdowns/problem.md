## The Problem

* You use a [password manager](https://www.nist.gov/cybersecurity-and-privacy/how-do-i-create-good-password#how-does-a-password-manager-work) and need to secure the main password.
* You own a [crypto](https://www.schneier.com/blog/archives/2017/12/crypto_is_being.html "https://www.schneier.com/blog/archives/2021/11/crypto-means-cryptography-not-cryptocurrency.html") wallet and need to secure the seed phrase.
* You [encrypt your hard disk](https://xkcd.com/538/) and need to secure the decryption key.
